About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated environments.

Through collaboration with universities and industry, IDR connects academic insight with real-world projects.

Why Institute of Digital Risk

🏢 Industry Led

🎓 Academic Collaboration

🚀 Deployment Focus

Training connects directly to industry deployment opportunities.
